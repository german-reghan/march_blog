26.2 Miles Later

December 14, 2025 was a day I had been equally looking forward to and feeling nervous about for months. My goal was to run my first marathon. The marathon I chose is Rocket City Marathon in Huntsville, Alabama.

Goal-Setting

My goals included:

  1. Finish the race without injury.
  2. Run the race in under 4.5 hours.
  3. Run the race in under 4 hours and 15 minutes.

I attained my first two goals but came in a few minutes after my final goal.

The Experience

The first word that describes the day was cold! The "feels like" temperature was seven degrees. The wind was literally blowing hats off of heads. Other than the bitter cold, the experience was amazing. Huntsville has a great, supportive running community. The course went all around town but the most memorable part was running underneath the rockets at the US Space and Rocket Center.

Lessons Learned

My big takeaway from the marathon was that I can do incredibly difficult things when I put my mind to it and put the time in. Training was the hardest part of finishing the marathon. There were many, many training miles to complete to be able to finish healthily. This required early morning wake-ups and choosing sleep over some fun evening events. Would I do it again though? Absolutely!
